研究方向：计算机体系结构、操作系统
Research Focus: Computer Architecture, Operating Systems

当前研究兴趣：
(i) 高性能内存/缓存系统：分层内存系统，内存池化; 新型内存介质(HBM, CXL, NVM)；稀疏张量加速器的缓存优化; (ii) 内存可靠性：内存故障预测与主动容错; (iii) 软硬件协同：地址翻译优化; (iv) 操作系统：并发控制,虚拟化

Current Research Interests:
(i) High-Performance Memory/Cache Systems: Tiered Memory Systems, Memory Pooling Systems; Emerging Hardware (HBM, CXL, NVM); Efficient Caching for Sparse Accelerators; (ii) Memory Reliability: Memory Fault/Error Prediction, Fault Tolerance; (iii) Hardware-Software Co-design: Address Translation; (iv) Operating Systems: Concurrency, Virtualization, Memory Management

I am actively pursuing research internship opportunities aligned with my academic interests. I warmly welcome experts from both academic and industrial backgrounds to reach out and connect. Additionally, I am deeply passionate about open-source community development and advancing computer science education.
